Problem & Solution

Why TicTac exists

The Problem

Classic game, terrible digital experience

When you want a quick game with the person right next to you, every digital option is buried under ads, requires an account, or feels like overkill for a 2-minute game. The simple thing shouldn't be this hard.

  • ✕ Ad-cluttered or app-store locked
  • ✕ Requires login or download
  • ✕ No way to track the running score
The Solution

Open the browser. Start playing.

TicTac loads in under 3 seconds and gets out of the way. No account, no ads, no distractions — just a clean board and a score tracker that keeps up with your rivalry.

  • ✓ Zero friction — open and play instantly
  • ✓ Score tracker across multiple rounds
  • ✓ Win animations & satisfying feedback
